    The Ultimate Ranking: Top 10 CFL Quarterbacks of All Time

    03 Oct 2024, 00:00

    As an avid sports fan, plunging into the storied landscape of Canadian football and counting down all-time 10-ranking CFL quarterbacks is both fun. This evolution has produced many names, from the legends who wowed Canadian football fans to those of more recent vintage who are still inspiring us all.    

     

    The Top 10

  • Warren Moon: The first name that graces this list is none other than Warren Moon, a CFL icon who led the Edmonton Eskimos to five consecutive Grey Cup titles from 1978 to 1982. Moon's remarkable talent and leadership on the field solidified his status as one of the greatest quarterbacks in CFL history.

  • Russ Jackson: Another name that echoes through the corridors of CFL history is Russ Jackson. His accolades, including the CFL's Most Outstanding Player Award and the CFL's Most Outstanding Canadian Award multiple times, place him among the elite quarterbacks of all time.

  • Damon Allen: Known for his versatility and exceptional skill set, Damon Allen's legacy in the CFL is defined by his remarkable achievements, including being the league's career passing leader at the time of his retirement in 2007.

  • Anthony Calvillo: Anthony Calvillo's enduring impact on the league is undeniable. His mastery on the football field, coupled with his longevity and versatility, makes him a stalwart in the pantheon of CFL quarterbacks.

  • Jake Maier: A modern sensation, Jake Maier's remarkable efficiency and poised performance on the field have propelled him to the upper echelons of CFL quarterbacks. His exceptional passing efficiency rating solidifies his place among the best of the best.

  • Trevor Harris: Returning from a serious injury, Trevor Harris has showcased his prowess by leading the CFL in passing yards and touchdowns, reaffirming his status as one of the premier quarterbacks in the league.

  • Cody Fajardo: With an impressive start to the season, Cody Fajardo has displayed his potential to ascend to greater heights in the CFL. His ability to lead his team to early victories underscores his impact as a top-tier quarterback.

  • Vernon Adams Jr.: Known for his electrifying style of play, Vernon Adams Jr. has consistently dazzled fans with his ability to turn the tide of a game with his trademark deep shots. His dynamic approach to the game cements his position as a formidable CFL quarterback.

  • McLeod Bethel-Thompson: Despite facing challenges in the early stages of the season, McLeod Bethel-Thompson's skill set and veteran savvy position him as a quarterback capable of engineering impactful turnarounds for his team.

  • Ricky Ray: Ricky Ray's enduring legacy and impact on the CFL cannot be overlooked. His illustrious career, highlighted by multiple Grey Cup victories and exceptional performances, cements his status as one of the top CFL quarterbacks of all time.

    • In the End

    These quarterbacks, spanning generations and eras, have collectively contributed to the rich tapestry of Canadian football, leaving an indelible imprint on the league. Their remarkable feats and enduring legacies serve as a testament to their unparalleled skill, resilience, and unwavering dedication to the sport.

    As fans, we are privileged to witness the continuing evolution of the CFL quarterback legacy, with modern stars carrying the torch while paying homage to the illustrious figures who paved the way for the league's enduring success.

    In celebrating the top 10 CFL quarterbacks of all time, we honor the spirit of competition, sportsmanship, and the enduring impact of these remarkable athletes on the storied history of Canadian football. Their contributions have not only shaped the game but have also captured the hearts and imaginations of fans across the nation, leaving an indelible mark on the sport that will resonate for generations to come.
