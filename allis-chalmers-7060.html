    Allis Chalmers 7060 Row-Crop Tractor featured an Allis Chalmers 7.0L 6-cyl diesel engine, hydrostatic power steering, 6,680 lbs [3030 kg] Rear lift (at 24″/610mm), 11530 to 20560 pounds weight, and 29.6 qts [28.0 L] oil capacity.

    Allis Chalmers model 7060 Row-Crop Tractor received the new Accousta Cab II with console shift, new seating, and new lighting. The tractor’s color changed from an orange chassis to black In 1978, serial number 6001.
