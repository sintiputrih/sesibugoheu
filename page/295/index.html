19 Feb 2024, 00:00

Anna Cockrell- Biography Anna Cockrell is a track and field sprinter and hurdler from the United States. Her collegiate team’s moniker is the USC Trojans, which stands for the University of Southern California. She won the Honda Sports Award for Track & Field in 2021, as well as the Pac-12 Tom Hansen Conference Medal of the Year. She is the founder and a key member of the University of Southern California’s United Black Student-Athlete Association.
