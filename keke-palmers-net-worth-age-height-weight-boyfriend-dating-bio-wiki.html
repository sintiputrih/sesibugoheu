    Early Life

    Lauren Keyana “Keke” Palmer was born on August 26, 1993, in Harvey, Illinois, to her parents Larry Palmer and Sharon Palmer.

    As a child, Palmer used to sing in the church. She got exposure after she performed in a stage show in the Chicago tourist destination.

    Personal Life

    In 2010, Keke Palmer was in a relationship with Rodney King. They also planned to get married, but they separated in 2013. She then started dating model, actor, and singer Quincy Brown in 2013. Their relationship ended in 2017.

    Palmer is also a philanthropist and works with the Boys’ and Girls’ Clubs. She has also worked with various other charities like Saving Our Daughters and YWCA.

    Age, Height, and Weight

    Being born on 26 August 1993, Keke Palmer is 27 years old as of today’s date 24th January 2021. Her height is 1.68 m tall, and her weight is 57 kg.

    Career

    At the age of 9, Keke Palmer auditioned for the stage production, ‘The Lion King’ in 2002. Two years later, she was seen in the film ‘Barbershop 2: Back in Business’. This was followed by appearing in ‘Akeelah and the Bee’. She then went to appear in the films ‘Cleaner’, ‘The Longshots,’ ‘Shrink,’ ‘Joyful Noise,’ ‘Imperial Voice,’ ‘Animal,’ and ‘Brotherly Love.’

    She has lent her voice in the film ‘Unstable Fables: Tortoise vs. Hare’ in 2008. She has also lent her voice in the films ‘Winx Club: The Secret of the Lost Kingdom’, ‘Ice Age: Continental Drift,’ and ‘Ice Age: Collision Course.’

    In 2004, Palmer appeared in an episode of the television series ‘Cold Case.’ In the same year, she was seen in the TV film ‘The Wool Cap.’ Her other television roles include ‘’ER’, ‘Law & Order: SVU,’ ‘Jump In!’, ‘Rags,’ ‘The Trip to Bountiful,’ ‘Just Keke,’ ‘and ‘Grease: Live.’

    She has appeared in the lead role of the television series ‘True Jackson, VP,’ ‘Winx Club,’ ‘Scream Queens,’ ‘Berlin Station,’ and ‘Scream.’ Palmer also had recurring roles in ‘90210’ and ‘Masters of Sex.’

    In singing, she released her debut album called ‘So Uncool’ in September 2007. Her second album, called ‘Waited to Exhale’, was released in June 2016. She has also released three mixtapes, 2 EPs, and ten music videos.

    Awards & Achievements

    In 2005, Keke Palmer was nominated in the Screen Actors Guild Awards. She has won two BET Awards out of five nominations and is the winner of the 2006 Black Movie Awards. She has also won one Black Reel Awards and has won 5 NAACP Image Awards out of 10 nominations. In 2010, she was nominated for the Kids Choice Awards. She is the winner of the 2007 Young Artist Award.
