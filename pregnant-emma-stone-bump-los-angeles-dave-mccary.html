    EMMA Stone showed off what appeared to be a large baby bump amid reports she's expecting her first child with husband Dave McCary.

    Emma, 32, was pictured cradling her belly as she walked with a friend in Los Angeles on December 30 – but she has yet to confirm widespread rumors that she's pregnant.

    The Hollywood actress was spotted wearing a coronavirus mask as she hiked around the area with a gal pal last week.

    Though the private La La Land star has yet to confirm the news, it appears from her recent outing that she is expecting a little one with husband Dave.

    Emma’s stomach was outlined in tight black pants, a black long sleeve shirt, baseball cap, and Adidas X Missoni sneakers as she strolled around Tinseltown five days after Christmas.

    A source confirmed Emma's pregnancy to Us Weekly on Monday, stating: "She seems very happy and very excited about becoming a mother.

    "She’s constantly oohing and cooing. She looks great, she looks healthy, she’s glowing.

    “She’s been keeping up with friends and work lately, she’s always staying active and gets her daily exercise in.”

    An insider also told E! News that Stone is "pregnant and just loves married life!" and the photos would appear to confirm this.

    Emma began dating her 35-year-old husband, who's a writer and segment director on Saturday Night Live, in October 2017.

    The couple got engaged in December 2019 as Dave announced the happy news with a selfie on Instagram.

    Fans speculated that the pair tied the knot in May when Emma flashed a wedding band on her ring finger while being interviewed by Reese Witherspoon.

    She sported the band, which had replaced her diamond engagement ring, during a chat featured on Reese's Hello Sunshine You Tube channel.

    It was believed that Emma postponed her wedding to Dave amid the coronavirus pandemic.

    They had been set to tie the knot in March in Los Angeles, but Page Six reported the wedding was cancelled.

    Fans on Twitter previously speculated that Emma was pregnant after photos emerged of her with a baby bump strolling through a neighborhood with her husband.

    In the photos, Emma was seen wearing brown overalls and a white shirt underneath concealing the potential pregnancy bump.
