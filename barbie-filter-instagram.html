    Here’s all you need to know about the Barbie filter

    Barbie’s filter is a fairly neat tool. The cast and crew of the upcoming comedy flick Barbie were quite active on April 4, 2023, releasing the second teaser trailer.

    In the Barbie filter, a person is depicted standing on a sparkly, colourful, rounded star shape. The performers’ names are displayed above their silhouettes, and a tagline is displayed below.

    Barbie is everything, according to Margot Robbie’s portrayal, while “This Barbie is a mermaid,” according to Dua Lipa.

    How to get the Barbie filter on Instagram?

    You must stop browsing Instagram and utilise the official Barbie Selfie Generator if you want to make your own version of the “This is Barbie” meme. Simply launch the selfie maker and select “Start” to get started.

    Immediately after, two choices will show up on your screen; you may either take a picture right then and there or just insert one from your camera roll. Once you’ve selected your image, the filter will turn it into a This is Barbie meme.

    Also, you have the choice of changing the snap’s size. The image can also be tilted in addition to being moved to the right, left, up, and down.

    How to customize your Barbie filter?

    You also have the choice to modify the colour of the star shape in the Barbie Selfie Maker to any shade of red, blue, pink, green, yellow, orange, or purple. A generated tagline will be provided, but you can modify it.

    The app will give you the opportunity to download your work once you have finished modifying and adding anything you want to your meme by tapping on proceed. You can post it as a story or a post on your Instagram profile.
