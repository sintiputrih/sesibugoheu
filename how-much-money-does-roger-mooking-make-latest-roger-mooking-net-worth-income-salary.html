    Roger Mooking is a chef, musician, and television host from Canada. Trinidadian by birth, Albertan by upbringing. Roger Mooking is the host of the Cooking Channel and Food Network Canada shows “Man Fire Food” and “Everyday Exotic,” both of which appear on the Cooking Channel and Food Network Canada. He also features on Food Network’s Heat Seekers with Aarón Sanchez. Roger has appeared on Today, Good Morning America, The Marilyn Dennis Show, Iron Chef America, and Top Chef Canada, among others.
