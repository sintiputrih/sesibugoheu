    Tim McGraw and Faith Hill’s three daughters are soaking up the sun on their tropical getaway, and Maggie McGraw looks like she may be paying the price after catching too many rays. The pair’s middle child, who is 22, shared a photo of herself sporting a pretty brutal looking sunburn on Jan. 4. “Lobster season bishes,” she captioned the pic, joking about her burned skin.

    Although Maggie’s sunburn looked quite painful, she seemed to be taking it like a champ, and looked relaxed as she held up the peace sign in her pic. For the photo, she rocked a teeny tiny black bikini, which showed off how the difference in her natural skin color and the post-burn skin. Several commenters urged Maggie to use soothing aloe on the sunburn, and many others also commented about how great she looked in her swimsuit.

    While Tim and Faith have mostly kept off social media during the family getaway, Maggie and her sisters (Gracie McGraw, 23, and Audrey McGraw, 19) have been sharing pics from the trip. Gracie has posted several swimsuit photos, including some of herself in the water on Jan. 4. Two days prior, Gracie also shared images of herself posing in the sand while rocking bikini bottoms and a white, crochet cover up.

    Faith was pregnant with Gracie when she married Tim in Oct. 1996, and Gracie was born the following May. The country stars were only together romantically for a few months when they tied the knot. In fact, they were both in other relationships when they first met in 1994, but there was simply no denying the spark between them!

    Now, Tim and Faith have been married for more than 20 years and are one of country music’s hottest and most successful couples. Plus, they have every right to be extremely proud of all three of their daughters — Gracie is a New York University graduate, Maggie graduated from Stanford University and Audrey is a student at the American Academy of Dramatic Arts.
