    Deanne Roberts Biography and Wiki

    Deanne Roberts is an American, Florida girl who’s excited to call Charleston her new home. She was born and raised in Tallahassee, Florida, and recently graduated with a degree in Broadcast News from the University of South Florida in Tampa, Florida. Go Bulls!

    During her time at USF, Deanne made her first on-air appearance at the age of nineteen while interning at WCTV Eyewitness News in Tallahassee. She also interned at Tampa Bay’s news leader, Fox 13 News, and reported and anchored USF’s student-run show, Florida Focus.

    If Deanne wasn’t in a news station or chasing stories on campus, Deanne was either running track, being president of her greek organization, volunteering in a student organization, or being a dog mom to her little Yorkie, King. She is a proud alumnus of the USF Women’s Track and Field Team. Have you noticed a lot of pink, green, and pearls in her outfits? Deanne is also a proud member of Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority, Incorporated, where she served as President of the Zeta Upsilon Chapter at USF for 2 years.

    When Deanne isn’t chasing the biggest stories for News 2, Deanne’s around town enjoying Charleston’s rich history, beautiful sites, and chowing down on some Lowcountry cuisine.
