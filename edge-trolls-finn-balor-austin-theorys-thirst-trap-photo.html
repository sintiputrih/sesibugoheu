    Edge stunned everyone by returning to professional wrestling at the 2020 Royal Rumble. It had been nine years since his neck ailment had pushed him into early retirement, but he came back and now the Rated-R superstar is on the last leg of his second return, feuding with the stars of this generation. Recently, Edge took a major shot at both Austin Theory and his current nemesis, Finn Balot over them flaunting their bodies.

    As he continues to hone his skills on a weekly basis on television, Austin Theory remains one of WWE’s rising stars. The rising star is now confidently billed as the United States Champion after establishing himself as a fighting champion. He also maintains excellent physical condition, as evidenced by a recent appearance with Finn Balor.

    Finn Balor remains one of the current favorite WWE Superstars in the organization. Furthermore, The Judgment Day leader also keeps himself in the top physical condition and has one of the most impressive physiques in the locker room.

    Austin Theory posted a photo of himself and Finn Balor together in the gym. The picture, which displayed their chiseled abs, showed just how jacked the two WWE superstars looked.

    However, this photo attracted the attention of the 11-time World champion, Edge. He took to Twitter, taking a shot at two of the brightest in-ring talents in WWE, trolling their apparent thirst trap for the internet.

    “Wow. You guys stand around the gym with your shirts off. Super cool.”

    Along with his wife Beth Phoenix, Edge recently defeated Finn Balor and Rhea Ripely at the WWE Elimination Chamber event. With months of the heated rivalry between the two, it seems apparent that Edge and Balor will collide in a mega match at WrestleMania 39.
