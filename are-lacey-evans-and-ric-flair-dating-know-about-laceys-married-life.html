    American professional wrestler, Lacey Evans broke the internet when the WWE started an on-screen romantic arc between her and wrestling legend Ric Flair. The on-screen couple has turned many heads. In fact rumours of them dating in real life have also surfaced but Lacey Evans really dating Ric Flair? 

    You might recognize Evans as one of the stars of WWE, she burst on to the scene in 2019 and became an integral part of the main roster in the subsequent years. As a matter of fact, she earned the Slammy Award and the Trash Talker of the Year in 2020. 

    In recent times, Lacey made confirmed that after she revealed that she was pregnant with a child on February 15, 2021, but “the Lady of the WWE” is in a relationship with someone else. 

    Who Is Lacey Evans Married To? Is She Pregnant? 

    Lacey Evans is married to Alfonso Estrella-Kadlec, her husband and partner of the last 15 years according to this source. The pair met when Evans was still a teenager and were married by the time Lacey was 19, the same age as when she enlisted in the Marine Corps. She spent 5 years in the military as an MP for the Special Reaction Team.

    They got married before she was posted away from home for military duty. She got interested in wrestling after interacting with a Staff Sergeant who also worked as a wrestling promoter on the side

    In their 11 years of marriage, they have one child named Summer. Again, the couple announced in February 2021 that they were expecting their second kid as per this site. They spent 5 years in a part-long distance relationship as Evans had to go all over the world as a Marine. 

    Alfonso Estrella-Kadlec is a construction business owner and a worker at his own firm. In addition to this, he is also part of the candle-making business that Evans started recently (in 2020). 

    Is Lacey Evans Really Dating WWE Legend Ric Flair?

    Ric Flair’s character in WWE is that of an eccentric superstar, he has had his fair share of relationships with colleagues over the years. In 2021, the WWE started a romantic storyline between the former champ and Evans. However, the pair are not dating in real life. The romance between Flair and Evans is all part of a storyline in the WWE. 

    Fans speculated that they were made an on-screen couple due to her friendship with Charlotte Flair. Evans was eliminated from the Royal Rumble by Charlotte Flair. In spite of this, Lacey helped Flair lift the SmackDown Women's Championship by attacking Becky Lynch. 

    Evans is in a relationship with someone else from an entirely different industry. They have been together for a number of years and share a happy married life together.
