    About

    Danish Actress and television personality who first rose to fame as the host of Celebrity Devil Race. She has also starred as Nanna Birk Larsen in the BAFTA-winning drama The Killing and appeared in the 2011 crime drama Headhunters.

    Before Fame

    In 2000, she moved to Los Angeles to train at the Lee Strasberg Theatre and Film Institute. Three years later, she moved back to Denmark where she spent a brief time as a radio host before earning her breakout role in Midsommer.

    Trivia

    She starred in the recurring role of Sif in the Danish comedy Isa's stepz.

    Family Life

    She was previously married to Danish Rapper Liam Nygaard O'Connor, otherwise known as L.O.C.

    Associated With
