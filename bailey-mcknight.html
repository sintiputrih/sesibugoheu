    Table of Biography

    Bailey McKnight is famous for appearing on her mother’s DIY hairdo YouTube channel. Bailey McKnight is also famous for being the twin sister of Brooklyn McKnight. The twins together create unique YouTube content exclusive to girls, twins, beauty, and fashion.

    Early Life and Childhood

    Bailey McKnight was born on 31 December 1999 along with her twin Brooklyn. Her mother is Mindy McKnight and her father is Shaun McKnight. Her age is 24 years old. Furthermore, her father left his previous job to manage the technical side of the family’s YouTube channels. Similarly, her mother is an established YouTuber who posts hairstyle tutorials on her YouTube channel.

    Similarly, Bailey has a total of five siblings. She has three biological sisters Bailey, Rylan, and Kamri Noel. She also has two adopted siblings named Daxton and Paisley. Her siblings often appear in their YouTube videos. Moreover, Bailey is an American national born in Allen, Texas, United States.

    Caption: Bailey McKnight with her mom and twin sister Brooklyn. Source: Variety

    Regarding Bailey’s education, she attends Baylor University in Waco, Texas. She along with her twin is majoring in Entrepreneurship. The twins announced their enrollment in April 2018.

    Professional Life

    The McKnight twins first appeared in their mother’s YouTube video in 2009. They appeared on their mother’s YouTube channel “Cute Girls Hairstyles”, in which she posts DIY hairstyles. The twins have had their own YouTube channel since 2013. Teen interest, fashion, beauty, and all things fun are the major focus of their YouTube content. 

    Likewise, the twins launched another YouTube channel ‘Brooklyn and Bailey‘ completely dedicated to “all thing twins” in November 2015. The channel’s title is “Squared” and it is also a web series. The series includes seven sets of twins from North America, Australia, and the United Kingdom.

    Moreover, Bailey and Brooklyn are also well-known singers. They debuted with “Dance Like Me” on 3 March 2017. Their first single also ranked in the top 12 most popular songs in the US iTunes chart. “SiMPLE THiNGS” and “What We’re Made Of” are other singles by the McKnight twins. 

    Furthermore, the twins also collaborated with Peter Hollins in August 2015  for the cover of the song “A Life That’s Good Can Be Bad” originally by Lennon and Maisy. Moreover, the twins launched their merchandise line in 2017.

    Brand Endorsements

    Apart from this, she has also done some brand endorsement jobs. She is the brand ambassador for the department store chain, JCPenny.

    Bailey McKnight – Net Worth 2024

    Everything Bailey has achieved so far is the combined achievements of the McKnight Twins. Hence, Bailey’s net worth is the same as that of Brooklyn, i.e. $2.5 million. Her net worth is influenced by her career as a singer, Youtuber, actress, social media star, and brand endorser.
