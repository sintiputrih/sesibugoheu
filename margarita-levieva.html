    Margarita Levieva is a Russian actress, voiceover artist, and dancer who has been cast in roles like Julie in the HBO comedy-drama How to Make It in America, Marcy Collins in the Fox serial drama Vanished, Abigail “Abby” Parker in the drama The Deuce, Natalie O’Connor in the NBC spy drama Allegiance, Gina Zanetakos in the NBC crime thriller The Blacklist, and Amanda Clarke in the ABC drama Revenge. She has also been a part of movies such as Sleeping with Other People, The Invisible, Noise, Knights of Badassdom, Inherit the Viper, It Happened in L.A., Future World, The Diary of a Teenage Girl, The Stand Up, and The Lincoln Lawyer.

    Margarita Levieva chose to study at a public high school in Secaucus, New Jersey even though she was accepted to study dance at Laguardia High School.
