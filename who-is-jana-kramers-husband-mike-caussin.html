    COUNTRY music singer Jana Kramer has been married to her husband Mike Caussin since 2015.

    On Wednesday, April 21, reports stated that Kramer filed for divorce from Caussin.

    Who is Mike Caussin?

    Caussin, 34, is a former NFL player for various teams.

    Prior to being drafted, Caussin played college football for James Madison University.

    After college, he was signed by the Jacksonville Jaguars in 2010 but cut later in the year.

    He then played for the Buffalo Bills but was waived by the team in 2013, then signing with the Washington Football Team a year later.

    He first started dating Kramer in August 201 and proposed to the singer in December of that year.

    The two wed in May 2015 and have two children; Jolie Rae and Jace Joseph.

    He and Kramer have a podcast together titled, Whine Down With Jana Kramer and Mike Caussin.

    They also released a book together in 2020 titled, The Good Fight: Wanting to Leave, Choosing to Stay, and the Powerful Practice for Loving Faithfully.

    The two briefly separated in 2016 after he admitted to cheating with multiple women.

    However, they renewed their vows in 2017.

    Who is Jana Kramer?

    Kramer, 37, is a country music singer and actress. She has appeared in popular television shows such as Friday Night Lights and One Tree Hill.

    She also appeared in season 23 of Dancing With The Stars and finished in fourth place.

    Kramer has been successful with her singing career with hits such as “I Got The Boy” and “Why Ya Wanna.”

    Before she married Caussin, Kramer was married to actor Johnathon Schaech; their marriage lasted 12 days.

    Kramer’s first husband was Michael Gambino, with the pair tying the knot in 2004.

    Kramer has been open about her marriage to Gambino and described the domestic abuse she went through throughout the course of their relationship in an interview with Good Morning America in 2016.

    What did Jana Kramer say about Mike Caussin?

    Kramer took to Instagram on Wednesday, April 21 to announce she is splitting from Caussin.

    The actress began in the emotional post: “‘It’s time.’ As I try to make sense of a reality I never wanted to believe could be possible again, those words have now become a reality.

    “I’ve fought y’all. I’ve loved hard. I’ve forgiven. I’ve put the work in. I’ve given everything I have, and now I have nothing else to give.”

    Jana continued: “Please know that I still believe wholeheartedly in marriage, love, and rebuilding. I just can’t fight any longer.  It’s time to heal.

    “Thank you for all the love, heart, and support, in many ways you have fought on this journey alongside me, and for that I am grateful. I’ll always encourage you to continue the good fight, but you can’t fight it alone.”

    A source told People that the split is due to the former athlete’s infidelity, which the pair have been open about in the past.
