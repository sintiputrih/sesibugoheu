    12 Feb 2024, 00:00

    A lot of celebs are taking over TikTok with their rising follower counts and the most entertaining content ever. TikTok users have been nervous about the notion of the app getting banned or shut down for the past several months but the good news is that it doesn’t look like TikTok will be going away anytime soon.

    RELATED: Joe Jonas Shares TikTok Video That Shows The Year 3000 And Nothing's Changed

    Other celebs aren’t interested in the phenomena of TikTok though, unfortunately. It seems like they might be missing out on all the fun! TikTok allows users to edit, crop, and manipulate video footage in very cool ways. Users can add filters, text, sounds, and anything else they want to add to make their content appear super cool and enticing. Several celebs have embraced the joy of TikTok and deserve to be followed as soon as possible!

    10 Embraced: Kylie Jenner

    Kylie Jenner has fully embraced the world of TikTok… the same exact way she embraced social media platforms like Instagram and Twitter. At first, she was floundering on TikTok since she was only posting boring content about her cosmetics line. She quickly figured out that to be successful on TikTok, she needed to post funnier content. She posted a lot of funny videos with her friends and some of them even poked fun at her older sisters.

    9 Embraced: Ashley Tisdale

    Ashley Tisdale has 5.4 million TikTok followers who have liked her content 59.7 million times. Her page is filled with dancing videos which makes a lot of sense because her bio says, “I’ve got moves you’ve never seen.” Well, we believe her! Ashley Tisdale still knows her moves from her High School Musical days with Zac Efron and Vanessa Hudgens.

    8 Doesn’t Understand: Mila Kunis

    Mila Kunis is fully anti when it comes to social media. It just doesn’t interest her. According to Entertainment Tonight, she said, "[The internet] took an ugly turn and became all about who can be the loudest, who can be the angriest and the most negative. Then it’s just not a fun game to play.” Social media really has caused a few problems with self-esteem issues of the younger generation over the years since it became so popular so Mila Kunis’ opinion isn’t too outlandish.

    7 Embraced: Ashley Benson

    Ashley Benson, who is currently dating rapper G Eazy, is embracing the TikTok game more than ever these days. She played the role of Hannah Marin on Pretty Little Liars alongside Shay Mitchell and Lucy Hale. On TikTok, Ashley Benson posts funny content with her friends and even shows off her fashionable side!

    6 Embraced: Ariana Grande

    Although Ariana Grande hasn’t posted a lot of content on her TikTok just yet, she is already getting the hang of it and embracing the app, much to her fans’ delight. With only two visible posts so far, she already has gathered 18.7 million followers and 34.1 million likes.

    RELATED: What To Expect From Ariana Grande’s Next Album

    If she is already doing numbers like these, there is no stopping her from her popularity growth on the social media platform. It is so exciting to think about what she is going to post next!

    5 Doesn’t Understand: Emma Stone

    Emma Stone isn’t a fan of social media and that includes TikTok. She told Elle, "It wouldn’t be a positive thing for me. If people can handle that sort of output and input in the social media sphere, power to them." It is unfortunate that Emma Stone doesn’t care to stay connected with her fans on social media because she would easily rack up the followers. She stays busy with her career as an actress so social media is not her top concern.

    4 Embraced: Demi Lovato

    Demi Lovato is an interesting celeb to follow because she has been through some major highs and lows in her life… like her recent engagement that got called off, for example. She keeps it real and is always open and honest with her fans about her life. Following her on all social media apps, including TikTok, just makes sense.

    3 Embraced: Katy Perry

    Katy Perry has been making her fans smile for years. Since her first single “I Kissed a Girl” premiered in 2008, she has been really living it up and thriving. These days, she is a new mom to Orlando Bloom’s daughter!

    RELATED: Katy Perry’s Latest Album ‘Smile’ Was A Commercial Flop, And Fans Think Her Career Is Over

    Her fans couldn’t be happier for her and that is why following her on TikTok is such an easy thing to do for people who adore her. Katy Perry has made such a huge impact on the world of pop music with all of her hit songs. Her TikTok account reflects her successes.

    2 Doesn’t Understand: Scarlett Johansson

    Back in 2011, Scarlett Johansson said, "I can’t think of anything I’d rather do less than have to continuously share details of my everyday life. I’d rather that people had less access to my personal life." Even though she had that opinion many years ago, she still stands by that same way of thinking today. She still has not downloaded TikTok… or any social media app for that matter.

    1 Embraced: Miley Cyrus

    Miley Cyrus is one of the most beloved celebs since her Disney Channel days as Hannah Montana. Her TikTok is one to follow for people who want to see her latest music, her best dance moves, and her most hilarious moments. She has 6 million followers at the moment and the number is only continually rising!
