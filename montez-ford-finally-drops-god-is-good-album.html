    Montez Ford Finally Drops God Is Good Album

    07 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Montez Ford has largely been a tag team wrestler, as part of The Street Profits with Angelo Dawkins. However, he has proven to everyone that he is a multi-talented person, dabbling in the art of music. In fact, he finally dropped his ‘God Is Good’ album as well.

    Montez Ford has been busy working alongside Angelo Dawkins and Bobby Lashley on Friday Night SmackDown lately, as they have been feuding with Karrion Kross’ new stable.

    With that going on, Montez Ford took to Twitter and announced that he has dropped his ‘God Is Good’ album on all major streaming platforms, including Spotify. The album has 8 tracks and even has a feature from former WWE Superstar Top Dolla, now known as AJ Francis.

    ”As promised, it’s finally here! GOD IS GOOD – ALBUM NOW AVAILABLE ON ALL STREAMING PLATFORMS! It’s been a wild couple of weeks, but thank you all for your overwhelming & continued support with our reality show & everything. I hope you enjoy! -Tezzie”

    Montez Ford is also confident he will be getting singles push in WWE soon. Nonetheless, fans of Montez Ford are certain to enjoy his latest musical project and that’s all that matters.
