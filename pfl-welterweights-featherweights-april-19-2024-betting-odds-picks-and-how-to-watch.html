    PFL Welterweights &amp; Featherweights - April 19, 2024, Betting Odds, Picks and How To Watch

    The PFL 2024 regular season continues with Week 3, featuring welterweights and featherweights in action for the first time this year. Together with Fan Arch, we'll cover where to watch the fight, discuss the betting odds for each fight, and make an intricate prediction on who we believe will emerge triumphant.

    06 Feb 2024, 00:00

    The PFL 2024 regular season continues with Week 3, featuring welterweights and featherweights in action for the first time this year.

    Together with Fan Arch, we'll cover where to watch the fight, discuss the betting odds for each fight, and make an intricate prediction on who we believe will emerge triumphant.

    Where to Watch

    • Where: Wintrust Arena, Chicago, IL
    • When: Friday, April 19th
    • Prelims: 6:30 PM ET on ESPN+
    • Main Card: 10:00 PM ET on ESPN and ESPN+ 

    Fight Previews and Betting Odds

    Main Card

    • Andrey Koreshkov vs. Magomed Umalatov; Welterweights

      • Betting Odds: Koreshkov (+135) vs. Umalatov (-165)
      • Analysis: Koreshkov, with his extensive experience and impressive win streak, is a formidable opponent. Umalatov's undefeated record, however, makes this a compelling matchup, with Umalatov being the favorite.
      • Prediction: Umalatov to win

    • Brendan Loughnane vs. Pedro Carvalho; Featherweights

      • Betting Odds: Loughnane (-505) vs. Carvalho (+380)
      • Analysis: Loughnane's past success in the tournament and his favorable odds position him as the clear favorite. Carvalho's recent losing streak may impact his performance.
      • Prediction: Loughnane to win

    • Logan Storley vs. Shamil Musaev; Welterweights

      • Betting Odds: Storley (-185) vs. Musaev (+155)
      • Analysis: Storley's experience and favorable odds make him the likely winner in this matchup.
      • Prediction: Storley to win

    • Gabriel Braga vs. Justin Gonzales; Featherweights

      • Betting Odds: Braga (-230) vs. Gonzales (+190)
      • Analysis: Braga, with his impressive record, is the favorite in this matchup, but Gonzales' odds indicate a competitive fight.
      • Prediction: Braga to win

    • Goiti Yamauchi vs. Neiman Gracie; Welterweights

      • Betting Odds: Yamauchi (-215) vs. Gracie (+175)
      • Analysis: Yamauchi's track record and odds make him the favorite, but Gracie's capabilities indicate a potentially close fight.
      • Prediction: Yamauchi to win

    • Bubba Jenkins vs. Kai Kamaka III; Featherweights

      • Betting Odds: Jenkins (-110) vs. Kamaka (-120)
      • Analysis: With almost even odds, this matchup is expected to be closely contested, with both fighters having a fair chance of winning.
      • Prediction: Jenkins to win

    Preliminary Card

    • Detailed betting odds and predictions are not available for the preliminary card fights.

    How to Watch

    • USA: ESPN and ESPN+ at the specified times
    • UK and Ireland: DAZN
    • Australia: Stan Sport

    Event Analysis

    The PFL 2024 Week 3 event features an exciting lineup of matchups with seasoned fighters and rising talents.

    The main card showcases compelling battles with clear favorites and closely contested fights, promising an engaging night for MMA enthusiasts.

    The preliminary card is also expected to feature intense bouts, adding to the overall excitement of the event.

     

    Ultimately, the PFL Welterweights & Featherweights event on April 19, 2024, presents an array of intriguing matchups and betting opportunities.

    With strong favorites and competitive odds, this event is set to deliver thrilling MMA action for fans and bettors alike.
