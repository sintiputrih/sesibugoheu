    Collector’s editions are awesome, but they have the unfortunate habit of sitting around and collecting dust. As someone who buys almost every one, I just wish developers got more creative with them. Luckily for gamers, the inFamous: Second Son collector’s edition not only comes with its fair share of completely random trinkets that you’ll look at once and never again, but stuff that actually has a physical use too, like clothes.

    Recommended Videos

    For a better understanding, take a look at this brand-new trailer that nicely outlines just what you’ll be getting if you so happen to choose to pre-order the collector’s edition.

    If you hate watching videos, here is a complete listing of the contents: a replica Delsin Rowe beanie, a vest case, 8 pins, official Department of Unified Protection patch, and bonus DLC titled Cole’s Legacy. In even better news, the collector’s edition does not cost an arm and a leg, and actually runs for a pretty reasonable price of $80.

    You can tear apart Seattle with an all new character and all new powers exclusively on the PlayStation 4 when inFamous: Second Son hits store shelves worldwide on March 21st.
