    OhGeesy’s bio: age, real name, children, net worth

    OhGeesy is a popular American rapper who, despite his fame, is known for keeping his private life personal, and as such little is known about him aside from his music side.

    OhGeesy is a member of the popular hip hop group Shoreline Mafia which currently as deal with Atlantic Record.

    According to the records, OhGeesy grew up listening to veteran rappers like 50 Cents who helped shaped his career.

    Over the years, OhGeesy has built a career for himself in the entertainment industry and is recognised as one of the most popular rappers in the US.

    This article is focused on what you should know about OhGeesy; continue reading to see more.

    OhGeezy’s Biography

    His real name is Alejandro Coranza, and he was born on the 27th of November 1993 in Los Angeles, California; there are no details of his parents, but he has a younger brother named Julien Coranza.

    The records shows OhGeezy’s parents are from Mexico, and so he is a Mexican American national; he would turn 28 in November 2021.

    Education

    There are no details on OhGeezy’s education history but he is believed to be educated.

    Career

    OhGeesy started his music career by uploading songs on SoundCloud, but he would later get a partner in 2012 after meeting Fenix while doing graffiti in high school. The two did some songs together and promoted it on SoundCloud. Some of their earliest songs together are Vice City, Tommy by Tha Gucci, Home Invasion, and Tha Field.

    The group that started with two members OhGeesy and Fenix, increased by two more members in 2012 after Master Kato and Rob Vicious became members.

    The four artists became a group and continued to record more songs, and named their group the Shoreline Mafia after Atlantic Records signed them.

    Some of the first songs by the Shoreline Mafia include Bottle Service, Nun Major, and Whuss The Deal. They didn’t gain recognition until 2017 when they released their first mixtape, ShoreLineDoThat Sh*t, which featured Musty.

    OhGeesy and his group members earned a number of accolades for their Off the Xtras (OTX) tour in 2018, and they included appearances at the Rolling Loud Festivals in Miami.

    Some of OhGeesy’s songs with the Shoreline Mafia include the likes of Nun, Major Bands, Pressure, Change Ya Life, Fell in Love, Gangstas & Sippas, Aww Sh*t Secret Service, Molly Water, Heavy, Perc Popper Bottle, Service Hoe Sh*t, Moving Work, Spaceship, Boot It Up, Big Bad Wolf, Mind Right.

    Personal life

    OhGeesy is currently in a relationship with and unknown lady, and they already have a son together named Sincere Juelz Coranza, who was born in March 2019.

    Net Worth

    OhGeesy is reportedly worth $2 million and he lives a comfortable lifestye in Los Angeles, California, USA.
