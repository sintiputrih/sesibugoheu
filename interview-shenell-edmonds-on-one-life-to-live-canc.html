    Sad news all around.

    Yesterday, the cancellation news of One Life To Live and “All My Children” devastated fans everywhere, as well as the cast and crew who delivered the shows to us five days a week. This morning the feelings are no different. This isn’t a bad dream but a reality we’re forced to face.

    Soaps.com spoke to “One Life To Live” actress Shenell Edmonds (Destiny Evans) who expressed, “Very sad news but I have enjoyed every minute of it and will continue to enjoy until the last day.”

    If you missed what Shenell’s co-star, the actor who plays former Navy Seal Brody Lovett, had to say about the cancellation find out through Interview: Mark Lawson On One Life To Live’s Cancellation.
