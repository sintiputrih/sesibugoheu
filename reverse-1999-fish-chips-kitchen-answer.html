    Besides fighting, you will also need to solve certain riddles in Reverse: 1999. Naturally, these can be pretty difficult to figure out. For example, let’s take the Fish and Chips in The Kitchen puzzle. This puzzle requires you to answer two questions: “Where are the fish and chips?” and “Where is the peas puree?” To help you complete this as quickly as you can, here’s the correct answer for the Reverse 1999 Fish and Chips in The Kitchen puzzle.

    Reverse 1999 Fish and Chips in The Kitchen Answer

    Reverse 1999 Fish and Chips in the Kitchen Thursday Answer

    First, let’s see the question itself. It reads: “The staff canteen is the best choice for filling the belly. Now you only need a bit more information to reach the fish and chips.” You will then proceed to a second screen which has two questions: “Where are the fish and chips?” and “Where is the peas puree?”. You need to answer both of them. The answer for the first question is Basket, and the answer to the second question is Cabinet. Note that the first letter of both answers needs to be capitalized. Otherwise, the game won’t register your answer. This is probably an oversight that is going to get fixed soon, but for now – your answers need to start with a capital letter.

    Reverse 1999 Fish and Chips Previous Answers

    The previous days – Monday, Tuesday, and Wednesday – serve as clues for figuring out the answer for Thursday. The only problem is that they can be hard to read. So here are all of them:

    • Monday: “Fish and chips in the closet. Bread in the cabinet. Peas puree on the shelf. Vegetables in the basket.”
    • Tuesday: “Bread on the shelf. Vegetables in the closet. Peas puree in the basket. Fish and Chips in the cabinet.”
    • Wednesday: “Fish and chips on the shelf. Peas puree in the closet. Vegetables in the cabinet.”
