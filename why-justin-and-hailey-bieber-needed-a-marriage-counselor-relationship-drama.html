    Justin Bieber and Hailey Bieber lived largely in the public eye even before their marriage. Their relationship often appeared flawless under the relentless scrutiny of the media's spotlight, but it can be all too easy to forget that even one of Hollywood's A-list couples faces their share of challenges behind closed doors.

    Even with Justin's music career and Hailey's skincare business, the couple are still facing intense media hate for their relationship. That is why seeking help from a marriage counselor was the first thing they did after tying the knot to make sure that they worked out their issues before navigating the complexities of married life.

    8 Justin And Hailey Bieber Asked For Help From A Marriage Counselor Right After Their Wedding

    After they tied the knot in September 2018, Justin and Hailey Bieber mentioned in an interview with Vogue that they had sought the guidance of a marriage counselor, underlining their commitment to addressing any challenges that arose maturely, rather than allowing them to fester and escalate.

    Their choice to consult a marriage counselor was a recommendation from their close friend and pastor, Judah Smith. Hailey acknowledged the hardship of marriage, she also shared her initial feelings of loneliness during their early weeks of marriage.

    7 Justin Bieber Had Commitment Issues That Made Him Unsure About Marrying Hailey Bieber

    Justin Bieber admitted on The Ellen Show in January 2020 that he initially struggled with uncertainties surrounding the marriage, primarily driven by his deep-seated concern about remaining loyal to Hailey Bieber. He shared how the concept of commitment had made him extremely nervous as he contemplated proposing.

    He recalled, "Am I able to make this commitment as a man and be able to honor, you know, what I say, you know because that's a serious commitment when you say you're gonna love someone for better or for worse and, like, be faithful. That's huge. Am I able to do that?"

    6 Justin And Hailey Bieber Struggled In Their Marriage Because Of Their Health Issues

    In an interview with Elle in March 2020, Hailey Bieber shared the challenges she and Justin Bieber faced in the early stages of their relationship due to his complications from Lyme disease.

    She recounted how outsiders were unkind and judgmental, unfairly critiquing Justin's appearance and speculating about his health, although they were both in the dark about his condition. Hailey further revealed that Justin is now in good health, but their journey together thrust them headfirst into the "in sickness and in health" aspect of their wedding vows.

    5 Justin Bieber Revealed That Getting Married To Hailey Bieber Was A Challenge

    During an interview with GQ in April 2021, Justin Bieber shared the challenges he faced during the first year of his marriage with Hailey Bieber. He shed light on the intricacies of their relationship stating that, "The first year of marriage was really tough."

    The singer elaborated that it was marked by the resurgence of past traumas and a struggle with trust issues. He acknowledged the difficulty of admitting such vulnerabilities to one's partner out of fear of scaring them away. Reflecting on that initial year as a husband, Justin described feeling like he was constantly walking on eggshells. However, over time, he transitioned from mere pretense to genuine belief.

    4 Justin Bieber Wished He Could Overcome His Trust Issues With Hailey Bieber Through Healthy Communication

    The media criticized Justin Bieber as someone difficult to talk to. However, during his interview with Vogue, he opened up about the difficulty he faced in trusting people and expressed his fear that individuals may have ulterior motives or lack genuine intentions.

    Justin reflected on his struggles and past relationship mistakes, which have shaken his self-confidence and judgment. He didn't shy away from admitting that even trusting Hailey Bieber has been a process marked by uncertainty. "We don't want to lose each other. We don't want to say the wrong thing, and so we've been struggling with not expressing our emotions..." he shared.

    Navigating healthy communication is an ongoing challenge, and in therapy, the couple is actively developing a dynamic that prevents their personalities from clashing.

    3 Hailey Bieber Recognized The Importance Of Therapy In Her Married Life With Justin Bieber

    Hailey Bieber openly discussed the role of therapy in her life. In a February 2021 interview on Women's Health UK's Going for Goal podcast, she shared how therapy has been an invaluable resource, especially in dealing with the relentless online trolling she encounters.

    "The way I've dealt with a lot of the negative attention is, I've talked it through with a therapist," Hailey acknowledged that her husband, Justin Bieber, has grappled with the intense scrutiny of his life in the spotlight for a longer period, offering her a unique understanding of the situation.

    2 Hailey Bieber Still Has A Lot To Learn About Her Husband, Justin Bieber

    In an interview with Elle in March 2021, Hailey Bieber offered a glimpse into her marriage with Justin Bieber, highlighting the constant process of growth and learning that defines their relationship. Hailey emphasized her unwavering commitment to their enduring love story. "I learn new things about him and about myself and about our relationship all the time…" she said.

    During the interview, Hailey also acknowledged the harsh realities of celebrity relationships, stating that success is never guaranteed, regardless of one's fame. She further elaborated that their shared faith as a couple served as a solid foundation, including seeking guidance through therapy sessions. "I'm not saying it's this easy-peasy thing that doesn't take work. We talk to a therapist. We do what we have to do." Hailey stated.
