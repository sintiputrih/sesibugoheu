    Peg Bean Obituary, Death – Peg McKamey Bean, a woman of remarkable beauty, talent, and unwavering faith, departed this earthly life on December 26, leaving behind a legacy of love and devotion. Born to the late Ed and Velma McKamey in Campbell County, TN, Peg embarked on a life journey marked by extraordinary faith. In 1959, she united in marriage with her best friend, Ruben, sharing 64 years of love, companionship, and cherished memories.

    A devout follower of Christ, Peg’s life radiated with a deep commitment to her faith. She was a fervent prayer warrior, known for her unwavering dedication to lifting others in prayer and selflessly serving those around her. Peg’s role as a mother, grandmother, and great-grandmother was a testament to her unwavering love and devotion to her family.

    She is survived by her loving husband, Ruben Bean, and their children: Connie Fortner (Roger) of Clinton, Sheryl Farris (Allen) of Rock Spring, GA, Michael Bean of Springfield, IL, and Brian Bean (Kim) of Knoxville. Peg’s adoration extended to her grandchildren: Brad Powell (Amy), Elijah Fortner, Nathan Farris (Brittany), Bethany Wooten (Mitchell), and Kristin Bean, as well as her great-grandchildren: Tyler, Addyson, and Lauren Powell, and Annie and Luke Farris.

    Peg McKamey Bean’s life was a reflection of selflessness, love, and devotion. Her enduring legacy of faith, kindness, and unconditional love will forever resonate within the hearts of those she touched. Her passing leaves a void, yet her spirit will continue to inspire and guide her beloved family for generations to come.
