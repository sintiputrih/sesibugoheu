    Ben Baller, an American Music Producer, Jewelry Designer, and Entrepreneur, has made a name for himself in the world of fashion and luxury. Born on January 27, 1973, in Los Angeles, California, Ben has built an impressive career in the music industry and established his own successful jewelry business.

    Rise to Prominence

    Ben Baller’s journey in the music industry began when he was offered a job by Dr. Dre, a renowned music producer. He started working at Dr. Dre’s Priority Records and later became a producer at Aftermath Entertainment. Throughout his career, Ben has produced over 20 platinum albums, showcasing his exceptional talent and expertise.

    In 2004, Ben took a significant step and co-founded his flagship business, IF & Co., along with his uncle and cousin. IF & Co. specializes in creating custom-made pieces of jewelry, catering to a high-profile clientele. Ben’s craftsmanship has been sought after by big names in the entertainment industry, including Snoop Dogg, Justin Bieber, Kanye West, Drake, and many others. He has even created a diamond-encrusted belt for the legendary Michael Jackson.

    Expanding His Business

    As Ben Baller’s reputation grew, he expanded his business ventures beyond jewelry design. He launched a clothing line called “Superism,” which further cemented his presence in the fashion world. Additionally, he entered the cannabis industry with the introduction of his own cannabis pen brand, “VVS Pens.”

    Profile, Parents & Early Life

    Ben Baller was born and raised in Los Angeles, California. His parents are Korean immigrants who settled in the United States. His mother was a prominent figure in the fashion industry, owning a jean factory. His father was an academic, working as a history professor at UCLA, specializing in Asian-American studies.

    Ben has two siblings. His brother, David Yang, is a video game developer, while his sister, Jeanne Yang, is a celebrity stylist known for her work with renowned figures like Jason Momoa and Lisa Bonet. Ben attended Beverly Hills High School and later pursued his education at the University of California, Berkeley, where he graduated with a BFA in cinematography.

    Personal Affairs, Spouse & Children

    In his personal life, Ben Baller has experienced various relationships. However, it was his encounter with Nicolette Lacson, a Filipino-American model and swimwear designer, that changed everything. After dating for a few years, Ben proposed to Nicolette with a ring of his own design. They exchanged vows in 2012 at Casa Del Mar in Santa Monica.

    Together, Ben and Nicolette have three children: two sons named London and Ryder, and a daughter named Kaia. The family shares a deep bond and enjoys the privileges that come with Ben’s success.
