    Stefany Johnson On MILF Manor

    It’s time for fantasies to come true. On MILF Manor,  older women who also happen to be mothers are dating younger men — almost half their ages! However, the MILFS aren’t simply looking for a good time, but rather a true love who’d be with them when they are even older.

    Stefany Johnson is one of the featured eight women who were searching for attractive younger men at a tropical destination.

    As per the network’s press release, “Although they may be a few years older than the average dating show contestant, these women are not out of practice. Ranging from 40 to 60 years young, this vibrant group brings their unique life experiences to the game.”

    Joining Stefany for the fun were her co-stars — Kelle (a real estate broker and wellness and fitness coach, and a mother of six), Shannan (an event planner for a health insurance company formally married for 16 years), SoYoung (a hairstylist turned surgical nurse who specializes in heart surgery), April Jayne (a “former heavy metal video vixen” who works as a personal trainer), Pola (a gym owner who didn’t date anyone for the past 9 years), Charlene (just come out of a toxic marriage), and April (an event planner and insurance rep with a strong work ethic and an even stronger personality).

    Among them, only Stefany and SoYoung have always preferred dating younger men.

    MILF Manor premiered on January 15, 2023, and it indeed was interesting to watch this relatively unexplored concept in the reality dating world. Unlike other shows, MILF Manor didn’t even highlight the couples’ age gaps for everyone knew that the dating pool consisted of men half their age, and also because the show believed the MILFS have decades of dating experience and will use it to find a lasting connection with men.

    Stefany Johnson Job

    Stefany Johnson is a real estate agent. In 2023, she was working at JohnHart Real Estate for over a year.

    However, Stefany already has over 3 ½ years of experience in the field. Back in Oct 2020, she even sold a $1 million property at 10646 Tuxford St Sun Valley, CA, 91352.

    So far, she’s served the areas — Sherman Oaks, Winnetka, Canoga Park, Northridge, Shadow Hills, Calabasas, Tarzana, Woodland Hills, West Hills, and  Porter Ranch — all in Los Angeles, CA

    Prior to entering the real estate world, Stefany excelled as a civil litigation paralegal in the legal world in Los Angeles. And thanks to that, she’s now successfully representing home sellers during dissolution and or probate matters. She has facilitated the process with the sellers and at times represented both the seller and the buyer.

    So, what sets this realtor apart? Well, it’s her “just do it” mentality that fuels her work ethic.  Her innate ability to be friendly with anyone provides her clients with unparalleled communication throughout their home-buying or selling process.

    Also, the star is popular for being a hard-working agent. Her exceptional customer service, professionalism, and willingness to work hard are unparalleled. “She strives to demonstrate the highest level of integrity and commitment. Stefany loves to rise to the challenge of helping people get the house they are dreaming of,” her realtor bio wrote.
