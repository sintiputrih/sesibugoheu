    Mila Kunis just took this classic school-girl shoe to new levels.

    Kunis, 32, donned a pair of platform Mary Jane heels while supporting husband Ashton Kutcher for his appearance on Jimmy Kimmel Live! on Monday, March 28.

    Though the Bad Moms star is usually in laid-back sneakers, her sky-high shoes were the perfect finishing touch to her monochrome ensemble. The head-to-toe black outfit featured a casual crew-neck top, matching denim skinny jeans and a leather-trimmed trench.

    Kunis kept the rest of her look low-key, opting for minimal makeup and texturized natural curls.

    Kutcher, 38, promoted his new comedy, The Ranch, on the talk show in a blue suit and black button-up paired with brown dress shoes.

    In the Netflix series, which debuts on April 1, the father of Wyatt, 17 months, plays a failed football star who returns home to help his family (including his brother, played by former That ’70s Show costar Danny Masterson) run their Colorado ranch.

    Taking a break from talking about the sitcom, Kutcher explained that one of his best accomplishments is his daughter. “It’s so good,” he said of fatherhood to Jimmy Kimmel. “It’s the best thing I’ve ever done.”
