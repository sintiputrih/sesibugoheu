    Talinda Ann Bentley was born on September 18,1976. While there is little or no information about Bentley’s early life, parents, or education, we do know that she was born and raised in Pheonix, Arizona. Bentley was apparently not born with a silver spoon, as she began supporting herself from a young age by doing a slew of odd jobs, including serving as a waitress at a burger joint, teaching elementary school students, and training animals.

    Bentley is probably a music lover like her. She attended the Los Angeles Music Academy, where she graduated in 1995. She then ventured into modeling in the early 2000s and eventually found considerable fame. Bentley came into the limelight when she was featured as a model in Playboy magazine’s College Girls section.

    Talinda Ann Bentley – husband, children

    Talinda Ann Bentley is the wife of ChesterBennington, the late Linkin Park singer. The couple met through a mutual friend, musician Ryan Shuck, in 2004 and began dating while Talinda was a model and Bennington was still married to his first wife Samantha Marie Olit. They tied the knot in 2005, shortly after Bennington’s divorce from Olit.

    Talinda and Bennington’s union produced three children, a son Tyler Lee and twin daughters Lila and Lilly. Talinda is also stepmother to three other sons – Jaime from Bennington’s previous relationship with Elka Brand, Draven from his marriage to Samantha Olit and Isaiah, an adopted son.

    The couple’s marriage was a seemingly sweet andenviable one while it lasted. After divorcing Chester, he claimed he was a broken man and credited Bentley with helping him overcome his battle with depression. According to Bennington, the divorce was one of the worst and best things that ever happened to him and left him so drained and bitter. In fact, the Linkin Park singer also dedicated the song some fresh air he needed at the time Give Me Your Name to his wife Talinda.

    However, after 12 years of marriage, rock fans, and the music world at large, were shocked when the news broke that Talinda’s husband had taken his own life. The seemingly committed husband and father, who had just returned from visiting family in Arizona, left his loving wife and six children and hanged himself at his home in Palos Verdes Estates, California, on July 20, 2017. Chester Bennington was alone at his home when he died and his body was discovered by an employee.
