    Singer’s Bio

    Alabama-born rapper Flo Milli, whose real name is Tamia Monique Carter, first gained attention on the internet for her viral freestyle videos on Instagram. She started rapping at 11 years old, inspired by Lil Wayne and Trina, and gained a following on SoundCloud before signing with RCA Records in 2019.

    Age and Personal Life

    Flo Milli was born on January 9, 2000, which makes her 21 years old as of 2021. She has not disclosed any information about her personal life, including her relationship status or any children she might have.

    Height

    Flo Milli stands at 5 feet 2 inches, or 157 cm, tall.

    Career

    Flo Milli’s debut single “Beef FloMix” was released in 2019 and quickly went viral on TikTok, eventually amassing millions of streams. Her debut mixtape, Ho Why Is You Here?, was released in July 2020 and received critical acclaim. The mixtape features her signature style of playful and confident lyrics over catchy beats, which draws inspiration from southern trap and bounce music.

    Top Songs

    Some of Flo Milli’s most popular songs include:

    – “Beef FloMix”
    – “In The Party”
    – “Weak”
    – “Like That Bitch”
    – “Pussy Cat Doll”

    Net Worth

    As of 2021, Flo Milli’s net worth is estimated to be around $1 million USD. This is expected to increase as she continues to gain popularity and release more music.

    FAQs

    1. Where is Flo Milli from?

    Flo Milli was born and raised in Mobile, Alabama.

    2. How did Flo Milli get discovered?

    Flo Milli gained attention on social media for her freestyle videos on Instagram and for her presence on SoundCloud. In 2019, RCA Records signed her to a recording contract.

    3. What inspired Flo Milli’s music?

    Flo Milli grew up listening to southern rap and was particularly influenced by Lil Wayne and Trina. She blends these influences with her own unique personality and style to create her signature sound.

    4. What is Flo Milli’s signature style?

    Flo Milli’s music features playful and confident lyrics over catchy beats with a strong southern influence from trap and bounce music.

    5. What is Flo Milli’s most popular song?

    “Beef FloMix” is Flo Milli’s most popular song, which gained viral popularity on TikTok.

    6. Has Flo Milli won any awards?

    Flo Milli has not yet won any major awards, but she has been nominated for several in 2021, including the BET Hip Hop Award for Best New Hip Hop Artist and the MTV Video Music Award for Push Performance of the Year.

    7. What are Flo Milli’s future plans?

    Flo Milli plans to continue releasing new music and has hinted at a collaboration with Megan Thee Stallion in the near future.

    8. What is Flo Milli’s message to her fans?

    Flo Milli wants to inspire young women to be confident, independent, and unapologetically themselves. She often uses her music to empower her listeners and encourage them to chase their dreams.

    9. What challenges has Flo Milli faced in her music career?

    As a young woman of color in the male-dominated rap industry, Flo Milli faced many challenges in gaining recognition for her talent. However, she persisted and has become a rising star in the industry.

    10. How does Flo Milli stay grounded despite fame?

    Flo Milli credits her family and close friends for keeping her grounded and reminding her of her roots. She also practices self-care and takes breaks from social media when needed.

    11. Who are some of Flo Milli’s musical influences?

    Flo Milli has cited Lil Wayne, Trina, Missy Elliott, and Nicki Minaj as some of her biggest musical influences.

    12. What advice does Flo Milli have for aspiring musicians?

    Flo Milli advises aspiring musicians to stay true to themselves and not let anyone else dictate their art. She also emphasizes the importance of hard work and dedication in achieving success.

    13. What is Flo Milli’s relationship status?

    Flo Milli has not commented on her relationship status, so it is unknown whether she is single or in a relationship.

    14. How has Flo Milli’s life changed since becoming famous?

    Flo Milli’s fame has brought her opportunities for touring, collaborating with other musicians, and gaining widespread recognition for her talent. However, it has also brought her increased scrutiny and pressure to maintain her public image.

    15. What can we expect from Flo Milli’s future music?

    Fans can expect more confident and playful lyrics over catchy beats from Flo Milli in her future music. She has promised to bring her signature style and personality to all of her upcoming releases.
