    Career

    Hailey Bieber started her career as a professional model and a television actress in the industry. She began her career by signing a deal with Ford Models and made her appearance in popular magazines like Tatler, LOVE, V, and i-D. In the year 2014, she made her appearance in the brand commercial of a renowned clothing brand named French Connection and walked the ramp for fashion show Topshop and popular designer Sonia Rykiel.

    In the year 2015, she did several photo shoots for popular magazines and brands like Vogue, Teen Vogue, Jalouse, L’Officier, Wonderland, Miss Vogue, W, Ralph Lauren, Tommy Hilfiger, and others. In the year 2016, she continued her campaign for these popular brands and magazines. After that, she signed a contract with a popular modeling agency named IMG Models in New York.

    Later in the same year, she has walked the ramps for a popular fashion house named Moschino along with popular models like Miranda Kerr, Jourdan Dunn, Chanel Iman, and others. In the following years, she rose to huge popularity and has worked with popular supermodels, fashion designers, and renowned brands and magazines. In the year 2017, she has also been featured in the promotional video for the Fyre Festival and in the year 2019, she becomes the brand ambassador of popular brand Levis Jeans.

    Along with her modeling career, she also made her acting debut with her appearance in the television documentary titled Livin It Unusual Suspects in the year 2005 and also in the TV show titled Saturday Night Live in the year 2009. In the year 2011, she has also made her appearance in the music video titled On My Mind by Cody Simpson, and in the year 2016, she has been featured in the music video titled Love To Love You Baby by Baptiste Giabiconi.

    In the year 2015, she has also hosted the 2015 MTV Europe Music Awards and co-hosted the 2016 iHeartRadio Much Music Video Awards in the year 2016 along with the popular model and host Gigi Hadid. She has also hosted the show titled Drop the Mic in the year 2017 along with rapper and co-host Method Man.

    She has also collaborated with popular fashion brands like The Daily Edited and Public Desire in the year 2016. She also announced the launch of a make-up collection in collaboration with a popular Australian brand named ModelCo.
