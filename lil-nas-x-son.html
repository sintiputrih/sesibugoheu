    Montero Lamar Hill, also known as Lil Nas X, has gained notoriety over the past few years. His song Old Town Road, which was released in 2019, propelled him to prominence.

    Fans have recently expressed an interest in learning more about Lil Nas X’s personal life. To see a clear picture of the musician’s 1-year-old son, keep reading.

    Lil Nas X reveals he has a 1-year-old son

    Yes, it is what you read. The singer of Lost in the Citadel used his Instagram Stories on Wednesday to inform his global audience of the good news. He even uploaded a photo of his youngster to the Instagram social media site.

    Lil Nas X stated, “Yes, I Have a Son and I’m No Longer Gonna Hide Him From This Cruel World,” in one of the Instagram Stories he uploaded. We have to admit that his youngster is a cutie.

    Not only that, but the rapper from “Am I Dreaming” also disclosed his son’s birthdate on the social media website. His son’s zodiac sign is Libra as he was born on September 27, 2021. He hasn’t revealed the name of his son up until this point.
