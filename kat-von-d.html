    Kat Von D Bio

    Who Is Kat Von D?

    Kat Von D, whose real name is Katherine Von Drachenberg, is a prominent Mexican-American tattoo artist, model, entrepreneur, and recording artist. She gained widespread recognition for her work as a tattoo artist on the reality TV show “LA Ink,” which showcased her exceptional tattooing skills and unique artistic style. Kat Von D is known for her distinctive appearance, including her heavily tattooed body and striking gothic-inspired fashion.

    Beyond her career in tattoo artistry, she has also ventured into other creative endeavors, such as modeling, music, and makeup. Kat Von D is the founder of her eponymous makeup line, Kat Von D Beauty, which has gained popularity for its cruelty-free and vegan makeup products. Her influence extends to her advocacy for animal rights and her involvement in various social and artistic projects.

    Kat Von D’s life and career have been marked by both successes and controversies, making her a prominent and influential figure in the world of tattooing and contemporary pop culture.

    Early Life of Kat Von D

    Kat Von D was born on March 8, 1982, in Montemorelos, Nuevo León, Mexico. This makes her 39 years old as of the writing of this article. Her zodiac sign is Pisces. Kat’s parents, René and Sylvia, were both German Argentines and Seventh-day Adventist Church missionary parents. She has a sister named Karoline and a brother named Michael. When Kat was just four years old, her family relocated to the Inland Empire, and she grew up in Colton, California. She received classical piano training from a young age, starting at six years old.

    Kat Von D credits her grandmother, Clara von Drachenberg, as a significant inspiration for her in music and art. The culture of San Bernardino County also had a profound influence on her tattoo artistry and style. Her musical tastes were shaped by bands like the Ramones and Misfits, which she started listening to at the age of 12. Her journey into the world of tattoos began when she got her first tattoo at the age of 14. She decided to leave school at the age of 16 to pursue her dream of becoming a tattoo artist. At the age of 15, Kat Von D spent six months at Provo Canyon School, where she alleges she suffered abuse. Notably, this is the same boarding school that Paris Hilton attended, and Hilton has also claimed to have been abused during her time there.

    The Career of Kat Von D

    Kat Von D decided to take a different path and dropped out of school a few years after getting her first tattoo. She embarked on a career in tattooing and eventually landed a job at Sin City Tattoo. Over the years, she honed her skills by working in various tattoo shops, including Blue Bird Tattoo and Inflictions. She eventually found her way to True Tattoo. During her time in the industry, she worked alongside tattoo artists like Clay Decker, Chris Garver, and Tim Hendricks. Kat Von D’s inspiration led her to appear on the TLC reality TV show “Miami Ink.” This opportunity arose when Darren Brass, one of the show’s regular tattoo artists, was unable to work due to an elbow injury. Kat Von D was a regular on the reality show for two seasons.

    With the financial support she gained from the show, Kat Von D was able to open her own tattoo shop, which she named High Voltage Tattoo. This venture also enabled her to launch her own reality TV show on TLC, titled “LA Ink.” Her fame continued to grow, and she even earned a Guinness World Record for the fastest 24-hour tattoo creation by completing 400 tattoos in 24 hours. Oliver Peck, her husband at the time, later broke this record by completing 415 tattoos.

    “LA Ink” was the show that catapulted Kat Von D’s career to new heights. It aired for four seasons but was eventually canceled by Kat Von D in 2011. In 2009, she published her book, “High Voltage Tattoo,” which performed well in the market, reaching the 6th spot on The New York Times best-sellers list that year.

    Personal Life of Kat Von D

    In July 2007, Kat Von D made the decision to stop drinking as her alcohol use began to jeopardize her work. An interesting fact about Kat is that she has tattooed herself with logos of various bands, including HIM, Misfits, Turbonegro, ZZ Top, Guns N’ Roses, AC/DC, Kent, Slayer, Mike Got Spiked, and “Slutallica,” a modified Metallica logo. She also made appearances in music videos for HIM’s “Killing Loneliness,” Alkaline Trio’s “Help Me,” GUNSHIP’s “Black Blood Red Kiss,” and “Black Leather” by the Cholo Goth band Prayers. Among her favorite musical artists, Kat Von D lists Lemmy Kilmister, The Mars Volta, and Selena.

    Kat Von D is a vegan, and her makeup line is cruelty-free. In 2016, she received Farm Sanctuary’s “Compassion in Action Award” for her work on behalf of animal rights. However, in June 2018, she sparked controversy by indicating on Instagram that she would refuse to vaccinate her future children and raise them on a vegan diet. But in March 2020, she recanted those claims, admitting that she had made a mistake and was “completely uninformed” about vaccinations. She clarified that she is “not an anti-vaxxer at all.”

    Relationships of Kat Von D

    Kat Von D has had several notable relationships over the years. She married fellow tattoo artist Oliver Peck in 2003, but they separated in August 2007 and finalized their divorce later that year. Afterward, Kat dated Alex “Orbi” Orbison from 2007 to early 2008, as documented in the first season of “LA Ink.” They even moved in together during one episode, but their relationship ended before the second season.

    From February 2008 until January 2010, Kat Von D dated Mötley Crüe bassist Nikki Sixx. She eventually started dating Jesse James, the motorcycle customizer, West Coast Choppers CEO, and reality TV personality. In August 2010, Kat Von D confirmed their relationship, and in January 2011, they became engaged. However, the engagement went through a series of ups and downs, with Kat Von D announcing their split in July 2011. Surprisingly, they later reconciled and got engaged again in August 2011, only to break up for good in September 2011.

    In September 2012, Kat Von D began dating Canadian music producer Joel Zimmerman, known professionally as Deadmau5 and even gave him a star tattoo beneath his eye to match her own. Their engagement was announced via Twitter on December 15, 2012. However, they too went their separate ways in June 2013, with Kat Von D citing Zimmerman’s alleged infidelity as the reason.

    On February 21, 2018, Kat Von D married artist Rafael Reyes, and in November 2018, she gave birth to their son. This marked a new chapter in her personal life.
