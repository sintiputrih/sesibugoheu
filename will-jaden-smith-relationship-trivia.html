    Will and Jada Smith have had an immense influence on their children's philosophical outlook on life. This hasn't always been to Jaden Smith's approval. Their mutual love for the stage has been something which has definitely cemented their relationship and however up and down their relationship might seem, no-one could disagree that they definitely love each other.

However, Will and Jada definitely seem to believe that Jaden is a cut above the rest and perhaps he is. It's hard to argue that his performance in Karate Kid 2 isn't something spectacular, and he definitely has some of that special stardust which makes Will a legend on screen.

    1 A Mutual Respect

    Despite having some turbulence in their relationship, father and son seem to express mutual respect for each other's careers. This was demonstrated when Jaden played The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air theme song at Will's 51st birthday celebration. This was a touching gesture, which Jaden added to by posting about his great and incomprehensible love for his Dad online.
