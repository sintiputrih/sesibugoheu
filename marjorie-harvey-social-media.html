    Marjorie Harvey Social Media: Marjorie Harvey, officially known as Marjorie Elaine Harvey was born on October 10, 1964.

    She’s a designer, stylist, and Instagram star. Marjorie is best known as the wife of American televisi on host, producer, actor, and comedian, Steve Harvey.

    Steve Harvey, officially known as Broderick Stephen Harvey Sr was born on January 17, 1957, in Welch, West Virginia, United States to his parents; Eloise Harvey (mother) and Jesse Harvey (father).

    Steve hosts a number of programs including; The Steve Harvey Morning Show, Family Feud, Celebrity Family Feud, and Family Feud Africa.

    He also hosts the arbitration-based court comedy Judge Steve Harvey, and he formerly hosted the Miss Universe competition.

    Marjorie and Harvey tied the knot in 2007 after the latter’s two failed marriages to Marcia Harvey (m. 1981–1994) and Mary Lee Harvey (m. 1996–2005).

    In June 2007, Harvey married Marjorie, who he says is responsible for making him a better man and changing his life. The couple have since then been together all this while.

    In August 2023, the couple hit major headlines following divorce rumors but at this time of writing (27 August), there’s no confirmation from either of them as to whether they are really divorcing.

    In an update of the event; Nigerian lawyer, philanthropist, and politician, Ned Nwoko has said that all allegations of conflict between Steve Harvey and his wife, Marjorie Harvey, were false.

    The Nigerian Senator and billionaire business mogul made this known on Saturday via his verified Instagram handle when he posted a photo of himself and his wife, Regina Daniels, and the Harveys.

    He wrote: “Fake news has been taken to new heights all over the world and this is so unfortunate. We experience this practically everyday and so when we read about our friend Steve Harvey and his lovely wife having issues, we had to contact him and he confirmed that he and his wife are very much okay and it’s all fake news and should be disregarded. The family is happy all together and we wish them all the best”

    Earlier, some blogs reported that Marjorie Harvey engaged in extramarital relationships with their private chef and bodyguard. The reports also alleged that she had filed for divorce and demanded $200m.
