    Is Boutique in English word?

    A boutique is a small shop, especially one that sells women’s clothes and jewelry. Boutique comes from a French word of the same spelling, which means any small shop. The word has its roots in the Greek word apotheke, meaning “a warehouse.” The same root word also gave rise to the English word apothecary.
