    Barry Sanders is a former professional football player in the United States. He was a running back with the National Football League’s Detroit Lions (NFL). In addition, he was recognized as one of the game’s best running backs. Every year, he was named to the Pro Bowl, and he was inducted into the Pro Football Hall of Fame in 2004.

    Yes, the first ten seasons of his career were spectacular, as he was the league’s top performer. He set an all-time NFL rushing record there, averaging over 1500 yards each season. However, he was released from the NFL in 1999.
