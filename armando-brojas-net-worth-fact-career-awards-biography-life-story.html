    Armando Broja is an Albanian professional football player who plays for Vitesse, on loan from Chelsea and the Albania national team as a forward. He started playing football for the youth team of Burnham Juniors. In 2007, he moved to the youth team of Tottenham Hotspurs and played until 2009. In 2009, he then moved to the youth team of Chelsea. On 26 February 2020, he signed his first professional contract with Premier League side Chelsea after agreeing to a two-year deal. He made his professional debut on 8 March 2020.

    On the international level, he has represented Albania at various age levels. He has represented Albania at U19, U21, and the senior level. He made his senior international debut on 7 September 2020.
