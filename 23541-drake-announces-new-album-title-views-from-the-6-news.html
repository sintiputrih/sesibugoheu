    Drake Announces New Album Title &quot;Views From The 6&quot;

    Drake's growing OVO roster will have a busy year, having already announced upcoming projects for Majid Jordan and PARTYNEXTDOOR, we now have the title of Drizzy's studio album to follow up Nothing Was The Same-- Views From The 6.

    07 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Drake's growing OVO roster will have a busy year, having already announced upcoming projects for Majid Jordan and PARTYNEXTDOOR, we now have the title of Drizzy's studio album to follow up Nothing Was The Same-- Views From The 6.

    You may recall Drake referenced something new from him would arrive in 2015 on "0 To 100" with the lyrics, "We already got spring 2015 poppin’ / PND droppin’ / Reps-Up P droppin’ / Majid Jordin droppin’, OB droppin’ not to mention me droppin’."

    Majid Jordan are set to release their new project A Place Like This on July 22nd via OVO Sound, distributed by Warner Bros. PARTYNEXTDOOR will quickly follow that up with his second project, PartyNextDoor 2, on July 29th. Along with these two OVO releases, Drake is also set to host the ESPYs on ESPN tomorrow, he's got his annual OVOFest in Toronto taking place in August, following which he immediately heads out on tour with Lil Wayne for the "Drake Vs. Lil Wayne" tour. On top of recording his new album, it's safe to say he won't have much free time.
